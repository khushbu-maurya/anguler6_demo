About Author

Coleman G Lauderdale

Coleman G Lauderdale is graduate of BlackHawk College in East Moline Ilinois.

Born In the City Of Chicago, He was raised In East Chicago Heights. Now Known as Ford Heights Ill.

Having served a little over eight years of his life in prison, He was wrongfully Imprisoned on two seperate occasions, for two seperate crimes.These experiences has given himan in-depth and broad perspective on the inner workings of social engineering that shaped and conditioned his life.While his story is semi-autobiographical, The correlation of many of his experiences is mirrored in the many other Black lives that matter.
